Rules For Reseller

1. You have to sell a minimum of licenses per month.
2. If the minimum sales target is not met, your reseller account will be permanently deactivated.
3. If the reseller panel is used only for personal purposes and not for selling, all activated licenses and the panel will be deactivated without notice.
4. Do not share your reseller keys' price or any other information with anyone. Violation will result in deactivation of all activated licenses and the panel without notice.
5. No full version keys will be deleted after creation. If you created keys in the panel, you have to pay for them.
6. We do not have a refund policy.
7. Create full version keys with genuineName, Email & Mobile Number details. Fake data will result in immediate deletion of the license.
8. Create the reseller account with genuine Name, Email, Mobile Number details. Fake data will result in immediate deactivation of the account and license.
9. We don't provide any full version keys to resellers for customer demo purposes. You have to buy keys for customer demo purposes as well.
10. Initially, we help you with support for your customers, but you have to learn it on your own and provide support for future needs.
